## Scope

- Keep durable shared specs here.

## Rules

- Keep single-file specs short and split when needed.
- Use `## Scope`, `## Decision`, `## Implementation Order`, and `## Decision Log`.
- Avoid scratch files such as `plan.md`, `task.md`, `handoff.md`, or `review.md`.
